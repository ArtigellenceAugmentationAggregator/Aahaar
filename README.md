**Balanced Diet** — nutrition + eating habits + eating **sequence**. The ancient science of eating, as set out in the Vedas and traditions across the world. *When* and *how* you eat, not just *what*. No Western or generic nutrition app combines all three.

## The Wedge

A **free 3-minute questionnaire → deficiency screening**. No login, no onboarding, immediate utility. One sentence:

> *A 3-minute screen that tells you what your food is missing — and what to swap.*

## Principles

- **No diagnosis, no prescriptions.** Aahaar guides; it is not a clinician.
- **Verified sources only** — WHO, peer-reviewed research, government data. Zero sponsored content.
- **Order is fixed:** natural first → supplements → pharma. Never the reverse.
- **No alignment** — not Ayurveda only, not Western only.
- **Edge-node privacy** — your health data never leaves your device.
